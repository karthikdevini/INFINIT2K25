About INFINIT 2K25

INFINIT 2K25 is the flagship annual event of the Department of Information Technology, Sir C R Reddy College of Engineering, Eluru, Andhra Pradesh. This event celebrates innovation, creativity, and technical excellence, offering a platform for students to unleash their potential and showcase their talents.

Packed with a variety of technical and non-technical events, this fest promises an exciting and enriching experience for all participants. Whether you're a coder, a gamer, an artist, or someone with a zest for fun and learning, INFINIT 2K25 has something special for you!

Event Date:March 12, 2025
 Registration Deadline: March 11, 2025, at 11:59 PM

Join us and be a part of this remarkable journey that blends technology, creativity, and enjoyment like never before. We can’t wait to see you at INFINIT 2K25!
